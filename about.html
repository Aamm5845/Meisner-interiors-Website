Our Story

Founded with a vision to elevate the art of interior design, Meisner Interiors has grown from a passion project to a recognized name in luxury design. Our journey began with a simple belief: that thoughtful design has the power to transform not just spaces, but lives.

Under the leadership of Aaron Meisner, our team has developed a reputation for creating spaces that balance classic refinement with contemporary innovation. From intimate residential homes to bold commercial ventures, each project tells a unique story across multiple continents.

With projects spanning from our home base in Montreal to the dynamic markets of New York and the culturally rich landscapes of Israel, our work reflects a deep appreciation for both local craftsmanship and global design sensibilities. We believe that design is not decoration — it's the art of building emotion into space, regardless of geography.
