ABOUT

We shape spaces
with soul and precision

Meisner Interiors is a studio devoted to crafting interiors that speak with quiet confidence and timeless allure. From private residences to bold commercial ventures, every project is imagined as a one-of-a-kind composition — sculpted by detail, defined by atmosphere, and alive with character.

Rooted in Montreal, our work balances classic refinement with daring creativity, blending local craftsmanship with a global sensibility. For us, design is not decoration — it's the art of building emotion into space.
